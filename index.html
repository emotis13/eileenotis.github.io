Walmart: Made in China

Stanford University Press, 2026

Based on years of ethnographic research in Walmart stores across China, Walmart: Made in China offers a rare inside look at how Walmart adapted its retail operations to China and how workers negotiated labor, legality, surveillance, and power while managing the ceaseless flow of commodities across the service floor.

“Eileen Otis’s ethnography of Walmart in China reveals how value is made—and contested—on the selling floor. A deeply original contribution that offers a rare, humane look at globalization from the shop floor.”

Gary Gereffi
Author of Global Value Chains and Development
