Eileen Otis

Associate Professor of Sociology and International Affairs, Northeastern University

Contemporary capitalism depends not only on producing commodities, but also on realizing their value. Through ethnographic research, I examine the labor, organizations, and inequalities that shape this critical juncture between production and consumption.
