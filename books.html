Books

My books explore how labor, gender, and consumption intersect in contemporary capitalism. Drawing on ethnographic research in China, I examine forms of work often overlooked in conventional accounts of political economy—from the market-embodied labor through which workers animate consumer markets to the realization labor through which commodities are transformed into profit.

Walmart: Made in China

Develops the concept of realization labor.

Markets and Bodies

Winner, ASA Sex and Gender Distinguished Book Award. Develops the concept of market-embodied labor.
